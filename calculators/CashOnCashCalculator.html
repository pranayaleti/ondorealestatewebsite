About Cash-on-Cash Return

What It Measures:

  • Annual return on your actual cash investment
  • Pre-tax cash flow from the property
  • Excludes appreciation and tax benefits
  • Key metric for cash flow investors
  • Helps compare different investment opportunities

Important Factors:

  • Higher down payment = lower CoC (but more equity)
  • Lower interest rates improve cash flow
  • Vacancy rates significantly impact returns
  • Property management fees reduce cash flow
  • Consider all operating expenses
