About DSCR

What It Measures:

  • Property's ability to cover debt service
  • Key metric for investment property lenders
  • NOI divided by annual debt service
  • Higher DSCR = lower default risk
  • Critical for qualifying for investment loans

Lender Requirements:

  • 1.20-1.25: Minimum for most lenders
  • 1.25-1.35: Preferred by many lenders
  • 1.35+: Excellent, best rates available
  • < 1.0: Negative cash flow, unlikely to qualify
  • Requirements vary by lender and property type
